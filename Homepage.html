Welcome

Welcome to Phifer Middle School's 8th grade digital art exhibit! This is where fellow 8th graders can post any type of art ranging from digital art pieces, to paintings on canvases. The students have complete and total creative liberties here as long as it isn't plagerism and remains school appropriate.
